FAQ

Why have I chosen to do an apprenticeship?

Whilst the traditional path of university has its benefits, I believe that apprenticeships, especially in the tech industry, can be a superior route as you have the opportunity to study and work simultaneously amongst professionals. With coding, I find that the best way to learn is to practice. Theory is important of course, but application is essential. I hope to develop my skills by taking on an apprenticeship.

What can I contribute to the firm?

My dedication. I am dedicated to my studies and my career. When I set my mind on something, I do what I must to achieve that goal. An example of this is very website. I am coding this website using HTML, CSS, JavaScript (and Jquery) just two months after beginning coding- just in time for my apprenticeship applications. I work well in teams and can communicate in four different languages. I am well-rounded and have above-average general knowledge.

What is my biggest weakness?

As I mentioned earlier, I'm a passionate student and I take interest in my studies and work. While it is certainly a strength, I would also say it's a weakness as sometimes I want to explore the various different parts and components of my work right away that sometimes I get lost. One way I overcome this is by taking a brief look, jotting down a quick plan and then getting to work in an orderly fashion.
